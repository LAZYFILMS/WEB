Lazy 100 - Color Negative Film

  • Daylight balanced

  • Respooled Kodak Vision3 50D stock

Lazy 100 is our daylight-balanced color negative film, carefully respooled from legendary Kodak Vision3 50D motion picture stock into 35mm still photography rolls. Built for those who love clean detail, soft tones, and cinematic color, Lazy 100 delivers some of the finest grain available in color film today.

Expect beautiful pastel colors, smooth contrast, and a gentle highlight roll-off that gives images a refined, timeless feel. Skin tones are where this film truly shines: natural, flattering, and full of life, making it one of the best films for portraits. Whether in bright daylight or softly lit interiors, faces render with warmth and elegance.

What makes Lazy 100 extraordinary is its insane dynamic range. Highlights hold detail beautifully, shadows stay rich and open, and scenes with difficult lighting remain balanced with ease. It gives you room to expose creatively while retaining that unmistakable cinematic latitude.

If you want ultra-fine grain, stunning skin tones, and rich professional image quality, Lazy 100 is a film you'll keep coming back to.

Best Results

  • Shoot in bright daylight for ultra-fine grain and clean detail
  • Excellent portrait stock with smooth skin tones and soft contrast
  • Handles high-contrast scenes with strong highlight retention
  • Best processed in ECN-2 workflow for intended motion-picture character
