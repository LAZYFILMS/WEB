Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship 35mm film all over India?

Yes. We ship every order pan-India from Mumbai. Most metro orders arrive in 2–4 working days; tier-2 cities in 4–7 days.

Is your film fresh / in date?

Our Lazy-branded film stocks are freshly cut, DX-coded and packaged in-house. Every roll is stored cold and shipped in protective packaging.

Can you develop my film after I shoot it?

Yes. Our Mumbai film lab develops C-41 (colour), E-6 (slide), ECN-2 (cinema) and traditional black-and-white rolls. We also offer scanning and prints. Mail-in service is available across India.

Which film is best for beginners?

Lazy 250 is our most forgiving daylight stock — pleasant colour, fine grain, and forgives over-exposure well. For low-light, try Lazy 500. For black and white, Lazy Monochrome.
