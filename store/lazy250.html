Lazy 250 - Color Negative Film

₹850

  • 250 ISO
  • Daylight balanced
  • Color negative

Lazy 250 is a daylight-balanced 250 ISO colour negative film with fine grain, huge latitude and a very forgiving character. It is equally comfortable on bright afternoons, overcast days and mixed indoor light with a bit of help from your meter.

With rich, natural colour and plenty of detail in both shadows and highlights, Lazy 250 is perfect for travel, portraits and everyday shooting. It scans beautifully and responds well to gentle overexposure, giving you soft contrast and a very classic film look.

Best Results

  • Rate at EI 200 for smoother shadows and a slightly softer look
  • Ideal for open shade, golden hour and soft daylight
  • Can be pushed to 800 for low‑light with more pronounced grain and contrast
  • Great all‑round stock for travel, portraits and street photography
