How It Works!

1

Fill out the form with your city, name, and other details. Suggest a cool spot for the photowalk in your city.

2

(Optional, but cool) Grab a few film rolls to show us some love 🫶

3

Wait a bit — we need at least 5 and no more than 15 registrations for a photowalk to be on.

4

Once we have enough people, you'll get an email with a 100% off film developing coupon for the roll you'll shoot during the walk, along with a local WhatsApp group link.

5

Get together with your fellow film comrades and decide the venue, date, and time.

6

Go shoot — all the corners of vintage cars, stone buildings, gas stations, and pastel clouds. Give us a shout-out through reels, stories, or posts if possible!!

7

Send your film back to us for free developing!

8

Then do it all over again next time 🌿

Some Terms & Conditions

We'd love for everyone who registers to actually join the photowalk — so please sign up only if you plan to go.

The free coupon is meant just for the roll you shoot during the walk. Please don't use it for other rolls or share it with others. We're doing our best to make these walks possible so everyone can get together and shoot more film :) Misuse of the coupon would really hurt and discourage us.

The coupon code is valid for one month after you receive it — so send in your film soon!

Each code covers only one film roll (we're trying to keep this sustainable for us!).

If you can't make it to the photowalk, just let us know via email. We'll happily pass your spot to someone else.
